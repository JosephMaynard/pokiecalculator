About Pokie Calculator

When you're out having fun, it can be difficult to keep track of all your winnings on the pokie machines, but now you can easily record everything with Pokie Calculator!

All of the data is stored on your phone, we do not record any information about your wins or loses. This does me that if you lose your phone all the data will be lost too, so try and look after it!

If you allow geolocation, you will be able to see on Google Maps where you got each win. The location data is also stored on you phone so we will not see where you've been!

Please note that if you delete your cookies, you may lose all of your saved data.

We do not in any way encorage gambling and if you find you are winning a lot less than you though you were, we would suggest that maybe you should cut back, or seek help if it becoming a problem.
